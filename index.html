H&M Auto Repair

Founded in 2003 by Humberto and Margarita Espinoza in order to follow Humberto's dreams of opening his own auto repair shop. With the support from the city of Huron and the community, H&M was founded.

In more recent years, H&M Auto Repair has been taken into the hands of Alfredo Espinoza, in order to remain a family business, with hopes of expanding it to include a shop in the city of Fresno too.
